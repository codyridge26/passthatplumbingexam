Vents & Venting — Quick Reference

Core Rules

  • Trap Arm Max length by diameter; maintain slope to vent takeoff.
  • Sizing By total DFU and developed length to vent/roof termination.
  • Min Sizes Many fixture vents 1‑1/2″+; verify table for groups.

Common Methods

  • Wet Vent Shared drain/vent within limits; bathroom groups typical.
  • Common Vent Two fixtures with connection at same elevation.
  • Island Loop Loop vent with return to vertical vent and accessible cleanout.

Inspection Traps

  • Vent too far from trap weir (exceeds trap arm limit).
  • Undersized vent on multi‑fixture branch.
  • Improper island loop or missing cleanout.

Practice with isometric diagrams to lock layouts.
